# Hotel-Booking-Data-Analysis-Python

**Project Overview**

This project analyzes hotel booking data to identify the key factors that influence reservation cancellations. Booking cancellations create revenue loss and inefficient room management for hotels. The goal of this project is to explore patterns in booking behavior and provide insights that can help hotels reduce cancellation rates and improve business strategies.

**Business Problem**

Hotels frequently experience booking cancellations, which leads to empty rooms and revenue loss. By analyzing historical booking data, this project aims to identify the main causes of cancellations and provide recommendations to improve pricing, marketing, and booking strategies.

**Research Questions:**

* What factors influence hotel booking cancellations?
* How do price and waiting time affect cancellations?
* Which booking channels generate the most reservations?
* Which months have the highest cancellations?

**Tools & Technologies**

* Python
* Pandas
* NumPy
* Matplotlib
* Seaborn
* Jupyter Notebook

**Exploratory Data Analysis**

The following analyses were performed:

* Percentage of canceled vs non-canceled bookings
* Comparison between city and resort hotels
* Monthly booking and cancellation trends
* Impact of ADR on booking cancellations
* Effect of waiting days on cancellations
* Booking source distribution
* Country-wise cancellation analysis

**Key Insights**

* About 37% of bookings are canceled, affecting hotel revenue.
* Higher room prices lead to higher cancellation rates.
* Customers with longer waiting days are more likely to cancel.
* 46% of bookings come from Online Travel Agents (OTA).
* Portugal has the highest number of cancellations.

**Business Recommendations**

* Improve pricing strategy during high-demand periods.
* Offer discounts or promotions to reduce cancellations.
* Reduce waiting time for booking confirmations.
* Encourage direct bookings through hotel websites.
* Improve service quality in regions with high cancellations.

**Conclusion**

The analysis shows that price, waiting days, booking channel, and seasonal demand significantly influence hotel booking cancellations. Hotels can use these insights to optimize pricing strategies, improve customer satisfaction, and reduce revenue losses caused by cancellations.
